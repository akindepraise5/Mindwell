How it works

Small check-ins that make a real difference.

Write freely about your day, your feelings, or anything on your mind in a space that's entirely yours and completely private.

03

Get gentle support

Our Promise

Built to support, not monitor.

MindWell exists to help you feel better not to create a profile, track your behaviour, or report to anyone.

  • Your private reflections are never visible to school administrators, lecturers, or staff.

  • Your mood check-ins and logs are personal to you no one else can see them.

  • Explore any wellness resource without worrying about read tracking or monitoring.

  • Any admin insights are aggregate and anonymous never about an individual student.
